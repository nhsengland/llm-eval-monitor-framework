Draft LLM Evaluation and Monitoring Framework v0.2.0

/

This framework presents a structured approach to evaluating and monitoring in the responsible use of Large Language Models (LLMs) in healthcare settings.
It is organised around three key groups:

  • - Suitability in Context
  • - Wider Impact
  • - Quantifiable Changes
with each containing critical dimensions for consideration.
The emphasis is on the practical implications e.g. how often to review each dimension, what decisions they inform, and what actions they drive.
